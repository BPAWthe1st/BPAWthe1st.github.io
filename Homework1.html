Pandas - The Most Amazing Animal

Why are pandas the best, you ask? Well, have you seen one? Besides being cute and cuddly, they are also very intelligent. They can be trained to ride bicyles and do other tricks, and they are very clever at figuring out traps to avoid getting caught.


Here are some facts you may not have known about pandas:

  • They have a pseudo opposable thumb that allows them to grasp bamboo
  • They spend 10-16 hours a day eating, and the rest of day sleeping. What a life!
  • Their black and white fur is actually great camouflage
