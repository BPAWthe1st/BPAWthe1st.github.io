SEO Tips and Tricks

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization and involves a collection of techniques for designing your website such that your intended audience can easily find it using popular search engines. Below are some best practices on how to implement SEO:

  • Choose a keyword that your audience with currently associates with your brand or you want them to associate with your brand
  • Use that keyword early on in your website (near the top and preferably in a header)
  • If you are using multiple keywords, only use one keyword per title.
  • Understand your competitors' use of keywords to design your site better (sites like Keyword Spy can help provide this insight.)
  • If you intend to use social media, add meta tags optimized designed for social media sites to ID key content from your site
  • Ensure your site has responsive loading times (checkout Google's PageSpeed Insights Tool)
  • Use internal links to establish an information hierarchy for your website and also help Google get a deeper understanding of the content on your page
